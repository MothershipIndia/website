Curated
Design Research

Collection and collation of info-graphical data derived through extensive research and content synthesis. This research encompasses myriad domains of inquiry including but not limited to art, literature, popular culture, social and political influences. This research repository serves as a guide to make informed and articulate design decisions, directly serving the fraternity of architects, designers, and individuals of diverse creative fields.

Immersive
Technology Development

Research, development and deployment of technologies that help further the discourse on virtual habitable realities. Applied innovation in technology helps evolve both in detail and functionality, the processes that govern spatial design communication. Consciously industry facing, we revolutionize real estate concept to key experiences, translating projects into interactive digital twins facilitated by continual exploration and application of evolving technologies.

Virtual Real-Estate
Experiences

Our virtual reconstruction and profiling of projects, delivered as custom applets and website-friendly interactive experiences, are indispensable to the AEC Industry. Allowing for cataloguing of all types of project data from layouts to 360 videos to neighbourhood mapping, unit and material selection inventories and more, our interactive suites allow for project evaluation, opportunity analyses and pre-sales to thrive even at an investment/ design stage. Circumventing the need for a physical sales flat, we catalyse a faster inception-possession trajectory for the real estate industry through unrivalled client engagement experiences.

Metaverse Architecture

Fantasy environments that are hosted on the Blockchain within which one can communicate, interact, and engage in a plethora of activities as a virtual presence of the self. An experience that is immersed in the ideation of digital scenography that stems from fictive or semi-realistic design concepts like fantasy realms in games, architectural capriccio, and spatial theatre. Creation of a habitable digital ecosystem, influenced by whimsy yet informed by real world applications where in you can work, play, learn, and someday live.

Digital Asset
Creation

An NFT is a digital piece of art that can be as simple as an Image or as complex as Virtual Real Estate. An NFT or as a collection holds intrinsic value based on aesthetic, utility and inherited value, attributes that can be monetized in the Web3 space by drumming up extrinsic value through hype and the right marketing strategies. Our research and ideation enables us to define new paradigms that give birth to generative NFT systems and/or conceptualizing thematic approaches that answers to an exciting new space, hitherto unexplored - a holistic deployment of Art and Technology informed by culture and necessity.

Interactive
Projection Art

Imagine your walls layered with fantasies – unfettered of the constraints of structure or dimension. Swirling colours in brilliant hues swirl and sway as they morph to forms of your conjuring, respond to your touch – teasing, tantalizing, darting - blurring the lines between imagination and tangible reality. Our Projection Art will enable your space to emote in ways restricted only by imagination, enlivened by bespoke experiences built on the bedrock of projection mapping, augmented and mixed reality technology, a truly unique installation art experience.
